The school

Dunamn High's compound is much bigger than that of my previous school. It has lush greenery around the school which is pleasing to the eye. It also has a resource media library that allows students to carry out their revision there. There are multiple science labs around the school for students to conduct practicals.

The school was built when there was a need to provide an opportunity for students who wanted to study in the midst of student-led protests in the many years ago.
